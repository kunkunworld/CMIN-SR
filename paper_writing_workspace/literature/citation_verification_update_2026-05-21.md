## Wording guardrails

- Do not write that CMIN-SR proves an MRW data-generating mechanism.
- Do not write that `lambda2_proj` is a guaranteed estimate of true
  intermittency.
- Do not write that the paper exhaustively outperforms classical multifractal
  estimators; only structure-function and MFDFA evidence are available in the
  current paper assets.
- Real Fama-French results are sanity checks only.
