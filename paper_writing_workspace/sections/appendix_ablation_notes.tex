\section{Ablation and Legacy Experiment Notes}

Recommended appendix organization:

\begin{itemize}
  \item Tiny CMIN: always-MRW bias baseline.
  \item CMIN-Robust: anti-confounding with heavy-tail, shuffled, GARCH, and regime controls.
  \item CMIN-SR v1/v2/v3: progression from scaling-only to monofractal competition and explicit curvature diagnostics.
  \item Boundary calibration: same-H raw contrastive calibration was insufficient.
  \item Raw zeta alignment: suppresses fake fGn/Gaussian curvature but over-linearizes MRW.
  \item Curvature-preserving zeta alignment: confirms the trade-off between fGn rejection and MRW curvature recovery.
  \item Finite-sample identifiability: deterministic estimators also struggle, supporting the limitation claim.
\end{itemize}
